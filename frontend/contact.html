Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about contacting Metanoia Foundation.

How long does it take to get a response after I submit the contact form?
We aim to respond within 1–2 business days. For urgent matters, please call the phone number listed on this page.
Can I volunteer or donate through this site?
Yes. Use the "Volunteer" link to learn about opportunities and the "Donate" button to make contributions.
Whom should I contact for media or partnership inquiries?
Please email our general address (info@metanoia.com) and include "Media/Partnership" in the subject line; we'll forward it to the right person.
Is my personal information kept private when I contact you?
Yes. We handle contact submissions in accordance with our privacy practices and will not share personal details without consent.
Can I request records or reports from your organization?
Yes — please use the contact form and specify the records or reports you need. We will respond with next steps and any required verification.
